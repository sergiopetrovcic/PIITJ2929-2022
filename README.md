## How to play
- How to operate the terminal tractor:
  - Move forward: W or ↑.
  - Move backward: S or ↓.
  - Turn left: A or ←.
  - Turn right: D or →.

- How to operate the reach stacker:
  - Move forward: W or ↑.
  - Move backward: S or ↓.
  - Turn left: A or ←.
  - Turn right: D or →.

- How to operate the ship-to-shore:
  - Move forward: W or ↑.
  - Move backward: S or ↓.
  - Turn left: A or ←.
  - Turn right: D or →.

- How to operate the ship:
  - Move forward: W or ↑.
  - Move backward: S or ↓.
  - Turn left: A or ←.
  - Turn right: D or →.

- Keys:
  - Tab: switches between predefined positions in the scene;
